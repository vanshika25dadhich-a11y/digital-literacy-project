Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, no matter how genuine the request looks.
- Don’t approve payment requests if you don’t know who sent them.
- Always double-check who you are sending money to before completing any transaction.
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links received through SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
- Only download apps from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or App Store.
- It is recommended to do not scan random QR codes, especially if they are sent by unknown people.
- Use app locks and enable two-factor authentication for extra security.
- Keep checking your bank transactions regularly to spot anything unusual early.

UPI / Financial Safety Tips

- One important thing to remember: you never need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
- Always confirm the receiver’s name before sending money to avoid mistakes.
