Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s
- Use strong and different passwords for your accounts.
- Keep your profiles clean and professional, especially on LinkedIn.
- Think before posting anything online, as it stays there permanently.
- Double-check information before sharing it with others.
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts.
- Be respectful while commenting or interacting with people.

Don’ts
- Don’t share sensitive details like OTPs, passwords, or bank information.
- Don’t post anything offensive, abusive, or inappropriate.
- Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown messages.
- Don’t accept requests from people you don’t know.
- Don’t share too much personal information like your location or routine.
- Don’t get involved in online fights or cyberbullying.
